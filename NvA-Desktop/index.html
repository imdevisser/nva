About me

My name is Irmarie, born in South Africa but grew up in The Netherlands. I've been an online Dutch teacher for over 4 years. My biggest hobby is traveling, getting to know new cultures and learning languages. I am currently learning Spanish. I am also fluent in English, Dutch and Afrikaans.


Because of my background and experience, I have a very good understanding of the types of challenges that Afrikaans speakers face when learning Dutch. Therefore, I know which aspects are best to focus on during the lesson.
